# IT Metrics Dashboard

## Overview

A Business Analysis portfolio project demonstrating end-to-end requirements analysis, stakeholder management, ITSM process improvement, and KPI dashboard design for an IT Metrics Dashboard solution.

This project was created to showcase Business Analyst capabilities across requirements gathering, business process analysis, ITIL-aligned metrics definition, and solution documentation.

---

## Business Context

**Organization:** TechServe Solutions (Fictional Organization)

**Department:** IT Service Desk

### Business Challenge

The IT Service Desk relied heavily on manual reporting processes, resulting in:

* 6–8 hours per week spent creating reports
* Limited visibility into SLA performance
* Delayed incident trend identification
* Reactive decision-making
* Inconsistent performance reporting

### Proposed Solution

A centralized IT Metrics Dashboard integrated with ServiceNow to provide real-time visibility into:

* Incident Volume
* Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR)
* SLA Compliance
* Team Performance
* Incident Trends
* Operational KPIs

---

## Business Objectives

### Objective 1

Reduce Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR)

### Objective 2

Improve SLA Compliance from 82% to 95%+

### Objective 3

Reduce manual reporting effort by more than 90%

### Objective 4

Enable data-driven operational decision-making
